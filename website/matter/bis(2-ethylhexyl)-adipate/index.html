bis(2-ethylhexyl)-adipate

matter

Has synonym(s): DEHA, Di(2-ethylhexyl) adipate

Definition: Bis(2-ethylhexyl) adipate or DEHA is an organic compound with the formula (CH2CH2CO2C8H17)2. It is the diester of 2-ethylhexanol and adipic acid. It is a colorless oily liquid.
