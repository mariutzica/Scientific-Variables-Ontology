heat_diffusivity

property

Definition: In heat transfer analysis, thermal diffusivity is the thermal conductivity divided by density and specific heat capacity at constant pressure. It is a measure of the rate of heat transfer inside a material. It has units of m2/s. Thermal diffusivity is usually denoted by lowercase alpha (α), but a, h, κ (kappa), K, and D are also used.
