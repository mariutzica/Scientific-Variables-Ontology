reach

phenomenon

Definition: A reach is a segment of a stream, river, or arm of the sea, usually suggesting a straight, level, uninterrupted stretch.They are traditionally defined by the capabilities of sailing boats, as a stretch of a watercourse which, because it is straightish, can be sailed in one reach (that is, without tacking).
