phreatic-zone

phenomenon

Has synonym(s): soil saturated zone

Definition: The phreatic zone, saturated zone, or zone of saturation, is the part of an aquifer, below the water table, in which relatively all pores and fractures are saturated with water. Above the water table is the unsaturated or vadose zone.
